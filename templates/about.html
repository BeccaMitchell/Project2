Why Slime in the Ice Machine?

Houston Natives may remember a nightly segment presented by the late Marvin Zindler of KRTK-TV Houston. Zindler was famed in the city for his self-described "Rat and Roach Report". During these reports he read details from his controversial City of Houston Food Inspection Program's restaurant reports on the air. The Rat and Roach Report soon became famous for Zindler's enthusiastic reports of "Sliiiime in the Ice Machine!"

Austin Health Inspection Scores vs Yelp Ratings and Reviews

For this project we decided to explore the most recent (2018) data provided by the local government of Austin, TX regarding health inspection scores on routine inspections for local restaurants and establishments. We were curious to see if patrons intrisincally recognized when establishments scored lower and if that affected or influenced the average rating on a popular consumer review site (Yelp).

We used Yelp's API key to pull in Yelp User reviews and compare against the City of Austin Health Inspection scores. We chose to create and display interactive visualizations for consumers to explore restaurants and scores in their areas throughout the city, as well as checking out their favorite restaurants. Throughout this project we explored using the following languages and tools--

Languages: Python, JavaScript, JSON, SQL, HTML, and CSS
Tools: API calls, Tableau, Excel, Pandas, SQLite, Heroku, GitHub, Plotly, Bootstrap
